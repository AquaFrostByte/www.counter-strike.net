[The CS FAQ]

Please consult this FAQ before e-mailing anyone on the team. Questions have been answered by both Gooseman and CLIFFE. If you have read this FAQ and still have a question, comment, or concern, feel free to e-mail CLIFFE.

I'm getting the error "could not load library C:\SIERRA\HALF-LIFE\/cstrike/cl_dlls/client.dll" -- What is going on?
This means that you don't have the latest Half-Life patch installed. Go here to download the latest HL patch file.

I'm getting the error "Hunk_Alloc:failed on xxxxx bytes." How can I fix this?
. CS uses more RAM than HL and TFC, so those with 32megs of RAM are having problems. In your command line, set -heapsize 32000 -- or try other values, smaller or larger -- depending how much RAM you have. If you don't know how to do this, right click on hl.exe and 'create shortcut' Right click on this new shortcut, choose 'properties', then edit the 'target' (command line) to say something similar to "C:\sierra\half-life\hl.exe" -game cstrike -heapsize 32000

Can I use images/graphics/pictures from counter-strike.net?
Yes! Provided that you credit "CS Team" and link to our site.

I'm having trouble connecting to games ever since HL 1.1 / CS 6.5. Will this be fixed?
Valve is currently looking into the connection problems associated with the new Half-Life version. The CS Team can't do anything about the connection situation.

Will CS ever support / include bots?
Yes, probably. However, bots will not be worked on until the mod is complete. Meaning, this is the last thing that will probably be done to CS.

When is CS going to be final?
Probably in a few months.

Can I join the CS team?
If we need any help, we'll post about it on the news page.

Why don't you release version 1.0?
Yes, probably in a few months. Version 1.0 will be an official HL update.

What role does Valve play in the development of CS?
Valve helps us with technical questions and general HL engine help. That's the extent of their help.

Who is RzE?
Rze runs our hosted site CSnation. He hails from California where he enjoys spending time with his arch nemesis MTG.

Can I be a beta tester for the next release?
Generally, no. We have a testing team that we trust assembled. It's mostly people who are friends or run a hosted site.

When will beta x.x be released!?
E-mailing us this question will not get you any more information than you can find on the main page. If it isn't posted.. we don't know. In fact, sometimes we don't know until hours before the release. If we know, we'll post it.

What about all this /me s---?
It's irc speak too.

Will you be hosting any more sites?
We are pretty much through with hosting sites, we feel the C-S.net "network" is sufficient and strong. However, by invitation we may ask certain sites if they are interested in hosting.

Is it true that Arcticcom [forum moderator and irc op.] has a fro?
Yes, quite so.

Why don't you trout slap people anymore?
Due to an e-mail from the SPCA threatening legal action, all trout slapping has ceased.

Do you have an irc channel?
Yes, you'll find certain members of the CS team there. The location is posted on the main page.

Why did you move away from Planethalflife/Gamespy?
We wanted to get our own domain and be able to host sites. PHL doesn't allow that. We left. Simple. It's not because they censored our interview during mod of the week.

Why did you choose to limit it to 20 players?
Any more than 20 players turns into chaos and your framerate drops.

Who is MTG?
MTG is notorious for spamming the CS forums many months ago. He has since turned into a CS community star. MTG lives with his family in Colorado and enjoys stealing things.

If I e-mail you suggestions, will you read them/use them?
We will read them but we can't promise to use them. You can also post ideas and suggestions in the proper forum.

If I send you CS news, will you post it?
Probably not. We want to keep the main c-s.net updates strictly about mod development progress. All general news should be sent into and will be posted @ CSnation. Contact Rze.

Who is Gooseman, where have I seen your name before?
He has worked on a few mods, Navy SEALS for Quake1 and Action Quake2. His role in Navy SEALS involved every aspect of the mod except for mapping and tech-f---ing-support. Getting back to coding is a welcome change since it allows him to have much more control over the mod content.

Do you need help with the web site? I make super l33t graphicz!!
We have it covered. If we need anything, we'll post about it.

I e-mailed you and you didn't respond... what's up?
Due to the volume of e-mail, all mails may not get a reply. All e-mail is read however.

Will you link to my clan/CS related site?
Again, contact Rze at CSN for news posting.

I'm having technical problems.. can you help me?
If you have a tech support question, we encourage you to post about it on the proper forum. Many people will be able to help you.

I want a [insert weapon name here] in the mod, will you include it?
We will probably not honor weapon requests.

Do you need any help with the Mod?
The one thing we need help with is mappers, all your mapping experts head over to the mapping specs page and help us out!

Can I send you a Pic Of the Day?
Our pic of the day is no more.

Why aren't you putting in hyperblasters and the like into the Mod?
If hyperblasters are your cup of tea then I suggest you wait for a SCI-Fi mod or better yet, Quake3.

Why did you leave the Action Quake2/Action Half-Life team?
Gooseman: I wasn't entirely motivated to make models geared to the Action them anymore. My models never really fit to well in Action Quake2 as it was. Leaving the A-Team was a hard decision and I still keep in touch with the members. They're a funny bunch of guys with a sense of humour not unlike my own. I just wish they were into military shit more. Hopefully though, our mods won't compete for a fan base since our mods have different enough themes. It's hard enough trying to get a fan base with all the other military style mods and games out there, I'd rather not have to compete with AHL and it's huge fan base.

Where can I download the mod?
See the downloads page for all that you'll need.

Where can I see screenshots of models and/or in-game shots?
All model and misc shots can be found at the weapons page, while all in-game screenshots can be found at the images page.

Gooseman, can you help me with modeling? How about mapping?
Gooseman: I'm too pressed for time to be giving lessons to be people who want to learn how to model. You're best bet is to visit www.planetquake.com/q2pmp You can find tutorials there and some models that would put mine to shame. Now scat ya lousy begger!
As for mapping, unless you want to know how to make Doom2 maps you're best bet is to ask someone else. I've never made maps for Quake2 or Half-Life.

What is your favorite Backstreet Boys song?
Everybooddyyy.. yeaahhh! Rock yerrr boooddyyy!! Yeahhhh!

Do any members of the CS team wear drawstring pants?
Yes. Some prefer gray coloured, yet others brown.

Do you support the HL Linux server port?
Yes, the CS linux server is released with each beta, usually on the same day and a few hours before the windows release!

I am a weapons expert and you did gun x all wrong, here's how you do it..!
Not every weapon will be perfectly accurate.

Which do you prefer: ding dongs or ho-ho's?
Dong Dongs.

The money system is messed up! I think it should be this way... will you fix it?
The money system will continue to be tweaked.

What's with the left handed weapons I've heard so much about?
All first person weapon models were strictly left handed up to beta6. With the release of beta6, you can now choose between left and right handed models.

Does Counter-Strike include Single Player? How about Deathmatch?
Just teamplay, multiplayer. CS does not support single player nor will it in the future.

Can I still submit a map for Counter-Strike although betas are already out?
Yes! We will continue to add new maps as the mod is developed and more releases are made. Head on over to the mapping information page for more stuff.

Will there be a site for Counter-Strike maps?
No. From my experiences of working for the Action Quake2 Map Depot, having one only makes the map situation for a mod a complete and utter mess. We are not going to delegate a map site for the mod. To get fresh maps out there, we are going to continue to accept new maps and they will be included in new releases of the mod. This will be a boon for everyone! It will make the map situation *much* more organized and save people *a lot* of headaches.

Will you release a mappack then?
Probably.

Where can I get info/screenshots of the included maps?
Head on over to the CS Maps page.
